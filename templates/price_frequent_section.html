Frequently asked questions

Our knowledge base contains answers to technical questions, and you can always contact us for more details on our plans, pricing, or any questions.

Can I try OrionScan for free?

Yes! OrionScan is completely free to try with our free plan.

If you have any questions, please contact us and we would be happy to discuss further.

What counts as a Scan?

A Scan is each time we successfully run a vulnerability scan against one of your servers or endpoints, which we call Targets.

For example, running OWASP ZAP against your web server example.com would count as a single Scan.

What counts as a Target?

A Target represents an individual IP address, URL, or domain you create as a target. Targets created from third party risks sources like Dependabot, or internal scans do not count toward your target limit.

How can we identify incoming traffic from OrionScan?

OrionScan maintains reverse DNS to scanners.orionscan.com for all of our servers, so that the IP addresses can be easily identified.

Our knowledge base has more information on how to get a full list of IP addresses.

Can I cancel my plan at any time?

There's no contract commitment so you can cancel at any time. Your plan will stop at the end of the current billing cycle.

Our knowledge base has instructions for how to cancel your subscription.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard and American Express via our payments provider Stripe.com. Plans can either be month-to-month or annual.
